## Seven Dwarves

**Time limit:** 1 second  
**Memory limit:** 32 MB  

Snow White prepares dinner every evening for the seven dwarves. One day, however, nine dwarves show up instead of seven. Each dwarf wears a hat with a unique number on it.

It is known that the numbers on the hats of the seven real dwarves add up to exactly 100. Among the nine given numbers, there is exactly one set of seven numbers that sums to 100.

Your task is to find and print those seven numbers.

---

### Input  
Nine lines, each containing a positive integer between 1 and 99.  
All nine numbers are distinct, and exactly one combination of seven numbers sums to 100.

---

### Output  
Print seven lines, each containing one number corresponding to the real seven dwarves.  
The numbers must be printed in the same order as they appear in the input.

---
