## Great Common Divisor

**Time limit:** 1 second  
**Memory limit:** 64 MB  

In mathematics, the greatest common divisor (GCD) of two non-zero integers is the largest positive integer that divides both numbers.

Given two integers, your task is to find their GCD.

---

### Input  
One line containing two positive integers `a` and `b`, where  
$$\[
0 < a, b \le 2{,}000{,}000{,}000
\]$$

---

### Output  
Print one integer: the greatest common divisor of `a` and `b`.

---

### Examples  

| Input | Output |
|-------|--------|
| `12 14` | `2` |
| `7 3` | `1` |
