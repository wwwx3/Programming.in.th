## ABC

**Time limit:** 1 second
**Memory limit:** 32 MB

You are given three positive integers representing **A**, **B**, and **C**. They may not be provided in sorted order, but it is guaranteed that:
[
A < B < C
]
Your task is to output the numbers in the order specified by a string of three letters (A, B, C).

---

### Input

* Line 1: Three positive integers corresponding to **A**, **B**, and **C** (not necessarily in order), each not greater than 100
* Line 2: A string of length 3 consisting of the letters **A**, **B**, and **C** (no spaces), representing the required order

---

### Output

Print the three numbers in the specified order, separated by single spaces.

---

### Examples

**Input**

```
1 5 3
ABC
```

**Output**

```
1 3 5
```

**Input**

```
6 4 2
CAB
```

**Output**

```
6 2 4
```
